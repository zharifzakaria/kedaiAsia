Our Story..

Our story began with the meeting of two friends, one from the sunny city Jeddah the other from the green metropolis, Kuala Lumpur. They both share a passion for good food and conversation, and in Jeddah they stumbled upon an inspiration - to bring together good food and delicacies from the four corners of Asia to a single destination.

Thus began their flagship store, an abode of flavours, delights and more. Come sample our wonderful products, the best Asian food experience, all here in Jeddah.
